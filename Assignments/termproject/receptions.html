Information about Receptions

Services and Amenities we provide for Receptions

Rehersal Dinners

Formal dinner or casual party, Temple Inn & Suites helps you plan and host a rehearsal dinner that’s as unique as you are. Trust our renowned hospitality and convenient planning resources to coordinate every detail, from the guest list and accommodations to the menu. From the perfect food to tempting desserts, it’s easy to plan a stunning meal to please any palette.

Receptions

Toast your new beginning and let the magic of the day continue. Take in the ambiance created just for you. Temple Inn & Suites helps you set up and coordinate every detail of your wedding reception. Our professional planners ensure your event is handled expertly, while our innovative dining options and stylish venues make your party unforgettable. From lush garden parties to grand ballroom dinners, Temple Inn & Suites provides a stylish, upscale setting for your wedding reception.

Dinner Menus

Have a taste for light hors d’oeuvres? Maybe a five-course formal dinner? Whatever you have in mind, our expert catering teams at Temple Inn & Suites work with you to plan a customized menu. It’s all about your preference. If a sit-down dinner isn’t your style, why not consider a casual party? The guest list can be flexible, too. Our helpful resources and event planning team make it easy to coordinate the details – so you can focus on enjoying your celebration.
