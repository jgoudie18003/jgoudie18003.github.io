Information for Full Time Temple Missionaries

Services and Amenities we provide for Full Time Temple Missionaries

Long Term Stays

You can call Temple Inn & Suites Home while on your mission. We work with the Church to provide long term apartments / rooms that meet all of the qualifications and costs as specified by the missionary department

Better than renting an apartment

We have amenities that regualr apartments don't have and we have specifically geared some of our services to your needs.As a resident you also have access to the Family History Center after hours, anytime access to the Temple Shuttle, and breakfast is still included with your stay.

For more information please contact missionaries@templeinnandsuites.com or call 1-800-YOU-KNOW
