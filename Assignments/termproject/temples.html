Soda Springs Avalanche

The road going up the big canyon was covered for 2 weeks due to an Avalanche that dumped 100 tons of snow on the road. Road crews worked tirelessly for weeks to remove all of the snow, debris and unblock the road. Fortunately no one was injured or killed in the accident due to this winters heavy snowfall.
